ABOUT US

Waguma Junior School Bamugolodde is a private primary school found at Bamugolodde Village, Bamugolodde Parish, Kalongo Sub-County in Nakasongola District, 62 miles from Kampala, off Katikamu - Wobulenzi along Bombo Road to Kikyusa Kazwama Road.

EDUCATION

We provide holistic education for Nursery and Primary Sectors. The school also offers education to some disadvantaged children as our meagre permit.

M.D.D

Waguma Junior School participates in Music, Dance and Drama together with co-curricular activities which helps to promote pupils' talents.
